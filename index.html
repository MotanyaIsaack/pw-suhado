Disaster Management

Disaster management is a key thing in addressing challenges facing internally displaced people and other abrupt humanitarian activities. Civic Education and Life Skills

The plight of the Internally displaced people and the general inhabitants of these communities at large are in dire need of life skills training on peaceful coexistence, management and coping up with the unfortunate events affecting their lives, responsibility with regards to family set ups and general knowledge that may lead to a good use of the available opportunities within their environment.

About Scaling Up Development Organization (SUHADO)

Scaling up is a non – governmental organization, based in Somalia. Somalia has experienced a wave of economic, social and political turmoil that has threatened the peace and tranquility in the region. This in turn destabilizes the economic potential and human rights needs within the region.

  • A lot of people have been displaced as a result of this, and livelihoods lost. Consequently, to ensure proper prosperity and humanitarian support of the Internally Displaced People (IDPs), Host communities, ensuring education and social justice, an initiative like this is the supplementary way of assisting the situation.

  • Scaling up was registered in 2019 in Somalia as an NGO focused on delivering support services to the people of Somalia, by the help of the founders together with the donors.

  • We work in collaboration with other agencies such as the United Nations and affiliated agencies, the local government, support institutions and individuals.
